Experiment 4 - Infinite Worlds - C

Description

Infinity
Basalt Columns are one of the naturally occuring hexagons in real life. This project has an infinite number of repeating basalt columns.
Variation in Space
The different columns have varying heights, colors, and locations. The water has varying color in specific locations.
Variations in Key
Changing the key changes the the color of the water, the position of tiles, the initial colors of the basalt columns, and the height of the basalt columns.
Signs of Life
Clicking a basalt column causes it to randomly shift in color. The water tiles move up and down with time.
